Open source CLI, hosted platform for teams. The CLI is the full product — nothing crippled. The platform adds persistence, shareability, team visibility.

**Why:** The monetization trigger is "someone who matters to your project can't use a terminal." Not team size — team composition. A technical founder + business founder = a customer on day one.

**How to apply:** Don't gate any CLI features behind login. `ana login` is additive, never a gate. Build sequence: proof cards first (acquisition feature), then dashboard (retention), then backlog + integrations (team tier). Don't build platform until 10 people have run the pipeline. The proof card design is disproportionately important — it's the marketing surface.
